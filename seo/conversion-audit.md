### Optimal CTA Placement Pattern

```
1. HERO CTA — Immediately visible, glow button, above the fold
2. CONTENT CTA — After the first major section (within first 30% of page)
3. MID-PAGE CTA — After educational content, before FAQ
4. END CTA — After FAQ, before footer
5. STICKY CTA — Persistent bottom bar on flagship pages
```
