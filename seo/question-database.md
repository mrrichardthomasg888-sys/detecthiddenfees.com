## How To Use This Database

### Question Scoring System
Each question is scored on three axes (1-5 scale):

| Score | User Intent Fit | Conversion Potential | SEO Opportunity |
|-------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 5 | Direct question consumers ask AI | User ready to upload document | Low competition, high demand |
| 4 | Common consumer search | User researching before action | Moderate competition |
| 3 | Related informational need | Indirect conversion path | Competitive but achievable |
| 2 | Broad educational query | Low immediate conversion | High competition |
| 1 | Niche or low-volume question | Minimal conversion path | Minimal opportunity |

### Coverage Status
- ✅ **Covered** — Existing knowledge article or page answers this question directly
- ⚠️ **Partial** — Question answered indirectly; needs stronger content or linking
- ❌ **Missing** — No dedicated content; new article needed
- 🔄 **Consolidate** — Multiple pages compete for same intent; merge or redirect

### Page Type Codes
- **KA** — Knowledge Article (question-based, in Knowledge Center)
- **TP** — Tool Page (analysis/upload page with SoftwareApp schema)
- **PI** — Pillar Page (flagship hub, e.g., ai-financial-advisor.html)
- **IG** — Industry Guide (industry-specific investigation, e.g., hidden-hvac-fees)
- **RE** — Resource Page (glossary, comparison, reference)
- **GAP** — No existing page; new content needed
