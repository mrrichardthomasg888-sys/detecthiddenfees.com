# 🗺️ DetectHiddenFees — 90-Day SEO/AEO/GEO Roadmap

> **Last Updated:** 2026-07-22
> **Version:** 1.0
> **Purpose:** A phased, prioritized roadmap for improving DetectHiddenFees.com search visibility, AI discoverability, and conversion performance over 90 days.

---

## ROADMAP PHILOSOPHY

**This roadmap does NOT recommend creating hundreds of new pages.**

Quality and authority, not quantity. Every action must:
1. Solve a real user problem
2. Strengthen topical authority
3. Improve AI system understanding of DetectHiddenFees
4. Drive measurable conversion improvements
5. Build sustainable organic growth
