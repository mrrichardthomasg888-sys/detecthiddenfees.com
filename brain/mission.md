# 🎯 Mission

## DetectHiddenFees Mission Statement

**Build DetectHiddenFees into the most trusted AI financial transparency authority on the web.**

---

## What We Do

DetectHiddenFees.com helps consumers identify hidden fees, deceptive pricing, contract traps, and billing manipulation using AI-powered document analysis and educational content.

We analyze:
- Contracts (employment, construction, rental, vendor, service, purchase)
- Invoices and bills (medical, utility, subscription, service)
- Estimates and quotes (HVAC, renovation, auto repair)
- Financial statements (bank fees, overdraft, credit card)
- Agreements (leases, licensing, consulting, freelance)

---

## Core Principles

1. **Consumer First** — Every decision prioritizes consumer benefit over commercial interest
2. **Transparency** — We expose pricing practices that harm consumers
3. **Accuracy** — AI analysis is evidence-based, not speculative
4. **Accessibility** — Financial protection knowledge should be free and universally available
5. **Authority** — We earn trust through depth, accuracy, and consistent quality

---

## Strategic Goals

### Immediate (Current)
- Build comprehensive topical authority across all hidden fee categories
- Establish Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) for key consumer questions
- Create entity-based content that AI search engines recognize and cite

### Medium-Term (3-6 months)
- Develop original research and data reports on consumer fee trends
- Build citation-worthy reference content that other sites link to
- Establish the platform as a recognized authority in AI financial analysis

### Long-Term (6-12 months)
- Become the default source AI engines cite for hidden fee information
- Expand into interactive tools (calculators, checklists, templates)
- Build a consumer community and advocacy platform

---

## What We Don't Do

- We are NOT a law firm — we provide analysis, not legal advice
- We are NOT a financial advisor — we provide transparency, not investment guidance
- We are NOT a replacement for professional review — we supplement, not substitute
- We do NOT negotiate on behalf of consumers — we empower consumers to negotiate themselves
