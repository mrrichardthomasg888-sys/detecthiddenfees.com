## Compliance Rules
1. NEVER claim AI replaces attorneys, accountants, or professionals
2. NEVER guarantee detection of all hidden fees
3. NEVER guarantee savings
4. NEVER create fake testimonials or reviews
5. NEVER create fictional team members or experts
6. ALWAYS disclose AI limitations on every AI-related page
7. ALWAYS include "not legal/financial advice" disclaimers
8. ALWAYS be transparent about data handling and privacy

## Consumer Protection Positioning
- Present AI as an informational tool, not a decision-maker
- Users make final decisions, AI provides information
- Focus on empowerment, not replacement
- Transparent about what AI can and cannot do
