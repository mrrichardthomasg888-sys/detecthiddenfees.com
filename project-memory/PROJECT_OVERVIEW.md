# Project Overview

## Websites
- **DetectHiddenFees.com** — Educational SEO authority platform covering hidden fees, AI document analysis, contract review, and consumer protection
- **HiddenFeeAI.com** — Commercial AI-powered document analysis service ($15 per document)

## Business Model
- **Revenue**: $15 one-time per document analysis via HiddenFeeAI.com
- **No subscriptions**: No recurring fees, SaaS model intentionally avoided
- **Lead generation**: DetectHiddenFees.com educates and drives traffic → HiddenFeeAI.com converts
- **PDF Guide**: Free premium-contract-guide.pdf provides lead magnet for email capture

## Target Customers
- Homeowners signing contractor agreements (HVAC, renovation, roofing)
- Car buyers reviewing dealership financing contracts
- Patients reviewing medical bills for errors/overcharges
- Consumers reviewing bank, credit card, subscription, and telecom bills
- Small business owners reviewing service contracts

## Main Conversion Goal
User uploads a document → AI analyzes for hidden fees → User gets report → User saves money

## Brand Positioning
- "AI-powered financial protection platform"
- Premium dark fintech aesthetic (not a generic blog)
- Professional-grade document intelligence for consumers
- Trustworthy, transparent, privacy-focused

## Competitive Advantage
- Specialized AI trained specifically on hidden fee patterns (not general-purpose AI like ChatGPT)
- Industry benchmarking across 12+ industries
- $15 price point vs $200-500/hr attorney fees
- Privacy-first: no document storage, no AI training on user data

## Long-Term Vision
- Become the #1 authority website for hidden fee intelligence
- Expand AI analysis to cover more document types and industries
- Build consumer negotiation tools and automated dispute resolution
- Advocate for pricing transparency regulation
- Partner with consumer protection organizations

## Brand Voice
- Professional but accessible
- Educational but not condescending
- Trustworthy (transparent about AI limitations)
- Consumer advocacy-oriented
- Premium without being elitist

## Core Values
1. Pricing transparency is a consumer right
2. AI should empower consumers, not replace human judgment
3. Privacy is non-negotiable
4. Independence from corporate influence
5. Accuracy and transparency about AI capabilities/limitations
