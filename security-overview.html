Security Overview

This page explains how DetectHiddenFees protects your documents. We encrypt everything, never train AI on your data, and automatically delete documents after analysis.

Encryption

All data is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ in transit and AES-256 at rest — the same standards banks use.

No AI Training

Your documents are never used to train or improve our AI models. Each analysis is a one-time, secure processing event.

Automatic Deletion

Documents are deleted from our systems shortly after analysis completes. We don't keep copies or backups.

Access Controls

No employee can view your uploaded documents. Access is restricted to automated processing only.

Third-Party Sharing

We never share, sell, or license your documents to third parties. Your data stays yours.
