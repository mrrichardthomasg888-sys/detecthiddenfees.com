Our Mission

DetectHiddenFees.com was created to help consumers navigate the complex world of billing, contracts, and pricing. Hidden fees cost Americans billions of dollars each year across healthcare, banking, telecommunications, insurance, automotive, home services, and subscription industries. Our platform uses AI-assisted analysis to identify potential fee patterns, unclear pricing language, and areas consumers may want to question before paying or signing.

We believe consumers deserve clarity about what they are being charged. By combining AI document analysis with educational resources, we aim to give people the information they need to make more informed financial decisions.

How DetectHiddenFees Works

Our AI-assisted document analysis examines bills, invoices, contracts, and estimates to identify patterns that may indicate hidden fees, pricing manipulation, or unfavorable terms. The analysis flags potential issues for review, explains what each flag means in plain language, and provides context to help users decide whether to take action.

DetectHiddenFees is an AI-powered information tool. It provides analysis and observations, not legal or financial advice. Users should verify any important findings with qualified professionals when making financial or legal decisions.

AI Accuracy and Limitations

Our AI is trained on thousands of documents to recognize common hidden fee patterns, pricing anomalies, and potentially unfavorable contract language. However, AI has limitations. It may not identify every issue, and it may occasionally flag charges or terms that are legitimate. New fee structures emerge regularly, and the AI may not immediately recognize patterns it has not been trained on.

We are transparent about these limitations. Our AI Accuracy & Limitations page provides detailed information about what our AI can and cannot do.

Our Approach to Privacy

Privacy is fundamental to how we operate. Documents uploaded for analysis are encrypted during transmission and automatically deleted after processing is complete. We do not use uploaded documents for AI training. We do not share document contents with third parties. Our full Privacy & AI Security policy explains our data handling practices in detail.

Our Editorial Standards

All content on DetectHiddenFees.com follows our Editorial Policy, which outlines our fact-checking process, correction procedures, and commitment to accurate information. Our Research Methodology page explains how we study hidden fees across industries using publicly available data, regulatory filings, and industry reports.

We do not accept payments or incentives from companies for coverage, positive mentions, or the inclusion or exclusion of specific information in our content. Our editorial decisions are made independently based on what we believe is most useful for consumers.

Transparency About AI

DetectHiddenFees uses AI to assist in document analysis. AI provides observations and patterns, not definitive conclusions. Users should:

  • Review AI findings carefully before taking action
  • Verify important charges or contract terms with appropriate professionals
  • Understand that AI analysis has inherent limitations
  • Not rely solely on AI for significant financial or legal decisions

For more details, see our AI Accuracy & Limitations and Security Overview pages.
