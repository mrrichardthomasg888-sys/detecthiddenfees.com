Internet Service Agreement Example: Hidden Fees in Broadband Contracts

This illustrative example shows how hidden fees appear in internet service agreements. Use it as a reference when reviewing your own service contract.

The Scenario

A customer signs up for a $59.99/month internet plan with a 2-year contract. The agreement contains several hidden fees and terms that increase the actual monthly cost to over $90.

Document Type

Internet Service Agreement / Broadband Contract

Pricing Breakdown

Advertised Monthly Rate: $59.99 Equipment Rental (modem/router): $12.00 Installation Fee (amortized): $5.83 Administrative Fee: $3.50 Network Enhancement Fee: $4.00 Taxes & Surcharges: $6.50 Total Monthly: $91.82

Potential Hidden Fees Identified

The following charges in this document may be inflated, unnecessary, or deceptive. Each item includes the potential overcharge amount.

Equipment Rental Overcharge

The $12/month modem/router rental costs $288 over 2 years. An equivalent device can be purchased for $80-120.

Potential Overcharge: $12.00/mo

Network Enhancement Fee

A $4/month "Network Enhancement Fee" that the provider can increase at any time with 30 days notice.

Potential Overcharge: $4.00/mo

Early Termination Fee (ETF)

The contract imposes a $240 early termination fee that decreases by only $10/month, locking the customer in for nearly the full term.

Potential Overcharge: $240 (potential)

Price Escalation Clause

The contract allows the provider to increase the "base rate" by up to 5% annually. After 2 years, this adds $6.15/month to the advertised rate.

Potential Overcharge: $6.15/mo (Year 2)

Important: These potential hidden fees are identified for illustrative purposes. Actual document analysis results may vary. Always consult qualified professionals for advice specific to your situation.

Questions a Consumer Should Ask

If you encounter similar charges in your own document, here are questions to ask the provider or service company:

Key Questions

  • • Can I purchase my own modem and router instead of renting? What models are compatible?
  • • What is the Network Enhancement Fee used for? Can this fee increase?
  • • What is the exact early termination fee schedule? Does it decrease each month?
  • • Is the price guaranteed for the full contract term, or can rates increase?
  • • Are there any other fees that could be added during the contract period?
