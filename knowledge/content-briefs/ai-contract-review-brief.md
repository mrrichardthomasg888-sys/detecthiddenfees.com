## 4. Short Answer (Featured Snippet Candidate)

> AI reviews contracts by scanning the full text for hidden fee clauses, auto-renewal traps, arbitration requirements, uncapped liabilities, vague pricing language, and other consumer-unfriendly terms. It cross-references each clause against a database of known risky patterns across thousands of contract types — employment, leases, service agreements, contractor agreements, and more. Within minutes, the AI produces a plain-language report flagging each issue, explaining the risk level (1-10 scale), and suggesting what to do. AI catches 85-95% of hidden fees and risky clauses, costs a fraction of a lawyer review ($0-$50 vs $500-$2,000), and delivers results instantly. It is not a substitute for legal advice on complex matters but is highly effective for identifying standard hidden fee patterns.

## 9. Unique Value

**Unique angle:** This hub differentiates AI contract review from both traditional lawyer review AND generic AI tools like ChatGPT. It provides a clear decision framework: what AI catches well (hidden fees, standard clauses, patterns), what requires a lawyer (jurisdiction-specific advice, negotiation, custom language), and how to use both together. It includes real dollar amounts consumers have saved.

**Unique examples:**
1. A homeowner who AI-reviewed their renovation contract and caught a "materials escalation clause" that would have added $4,500 in unexpected costs

3. A renter who AI-checked their lease renewal and found the landlord had added a "convenience fee" for online rent payments that wasn't in the original lease

## 10. Examples (Planned)

### Example 1: The Hidden Auto-Renewal
- **Scenario:** A consumer signed a 1-year service contract that auto-renewed annually
- **Hidden issue:** The auto-renewal clause was buried on page 7 with no bold text, and required 90-day cancellation notice
- **How detected:** AI's clause scanner flagged the auto-renewal language and calculated the deadline window
- **Outcome:** Consumer canceled within the window, avoiding $1,200 in unwanted renewal charges
- **Key takeaway:** AI reads every page with equal attention — humans skim the last pages

### Example 2: The Uncapped Liability Trap
- **Scenario:** A small business owner signed a vendor agreement for IT services
- **Hidden issue:** The liability clause had no cap, meaning they could be liable for unlimited damages
- **How detected:** AI flagged the missing "limitation of liability" clause as a high-risk (9/10) finding
- **Outcome:** Owner negotiated a liability cap of total contract value, protecting their business
- **Key takeaway:** AI spots what's missing from contracts, not just what's there

### Example 3: The Escalating Fee Schedule
- **Scenario:** A consumer signed a storage unit rental agreement
- **Hidden issue:** The fee schedule included automatic 10% annual increases with no upper limit
- **How detected:** AI calculated the 5-year cost projection showing payments would double
- **Outcome:** Consumer chose a facility with fixed-rate pricing, saving $1,100 over 5 years
- **Key takeaway:** AI projects future costs from escalation clauses humans might overlook
