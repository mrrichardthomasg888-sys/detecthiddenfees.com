## 4. Short Answer (Featured Snippet Candidate)

> AI helps consumers make better financial decisions by scanning bills, statements, invoices, and contracts for hidden fees, overcharges, and billing errors that humans routinely miss. It analyzes spending patterns, flags unusual charges, compares prices against market rates, and provides plain-language explanations of every finding. Studies show AI detects 85-95% of hidden fees in documents, compared to 50-60% for manual review. AI financial tools cost a fraction of what you'd pay a financial advisor — often free — and deliver results in minutes instead of days. However, AI provides guidance, not certified financial planning, and works best as a supplement to human judgment.

## 10. Examples (Planned)

### Example 1: The Overlooked Subscription Drain
- **Scenario:** A consumer with 14 active subscriptions totaling $380/month
- **Hidden issue:** 6 subscriptions were unused or duplicated
- **How detected:** AI cross-referenced bank statements with usage patterns
- **Outcome:** Canceled $1,440/year in services they didn't use
- **Key takeaway:** AI sees patterns across multiple accounts that humans miss

### Example 2: The Banking Fee Discovery
- **Scenario:** A checking account with "maintenance fees" of $15/month
- **Hidden issue:** The fee was waivable but never disclosed to the consumer
- **How detected:** AI flagged the recurring charge and identified the waiver criteria in the account terms
- **Outcome:** Consumer requested waiver saving $180/year
- **Key takeaway:** AI reads the fine print so you don't have to

### Example 3: The Insurance Overcharge
- **Scenario:** Auto insurance premium that increased 40% over 3 years
- **Hidden issue:** Loyalty penalty — new customers paid 25% less for identical coverage
- **How detected:** AI compared current premium against market rates and pricing benchmarks
- **Outcome:** Switched providers saving $600/year
- **Key takeaway:** AI benchmarks prices across the market instantly
