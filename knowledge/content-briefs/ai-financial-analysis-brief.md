## 4. Short Answer (Featured Snippet Candidate)

> AI analyzes financial documents by extracting text from PDFs, images, and scanned documents using optical character recognition (OCR), then scanning for pricing patterns, unusual charges, duplicate entries, and known hidden fee types. It cross-references found charges against market benchmarks, flagging anything above normal ranges. The AI identifies billing errors like double-charges, expired promotional rates, unauthorized fees, and subscription bloat — then presents findings in plain language with specific amounts and recommended actions. Most consumers who use AI financial analysis discover $200-$800 in recoverable overcharges on their first scan. The process takes 2-5 minutes per document and costs nothing compared to manual review that takes hours.

## 10. Examples (Planned)

### Example 1: The Medical Bill Audit
- **Scenario:** A consumer received a $3,200 hospital bill they couldn't understand
- **Hidden issue:** Duplicate charges for lab work, an "administrative fee" not in their insurance agreement, and a facility fee that should have been waived
- **How detected:** AI compared each line item against standard billing codes and the patient's insurance plan
- **Outcome:** Reduced the bill to $1,850 — saving $1,350
- **Key takeaway:** AI catches medical billing errors that even hospital billing departments miss

### Example 2: The Utility Overcharge
- **Scenario:** Monthly electric bills that crept up 60% over 2 years
- **Hidden issue:** An expired promotional rate auto-renewed at a 40% higher base rate
- **How detected:** AI flagged the rate increase and compared it against available plans
- **Outcome:** Negotiated a new plan saving $420/year
- **Key takeaway:** AI detects rate changes that happen gradually over months

### Example 3: The Credit Card Fee Pattern
- **Scenario:** A credit card with $279/year in annual fees, foreign transaction fees, and late payment fees
- **Hidden issue:** The annual fee had increased from $95 to $279 over 3 years without clear notification
- **How detected:** AI flagged the increasing fee pattern and compared it against cards with similar benefits
- **Outcome:** Consumer switched to a no-annual-fee card and saved $279/year
- **Key takeaway:** AI spots fee escalation patterns across billing cycles
