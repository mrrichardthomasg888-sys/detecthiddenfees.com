Hidden Fee Dictionary: Complete A-to-Z Guide to Fee Terminology

This dictionary defines 76+ hidden fee terms. Each entry includes the definition, typical cost range, common industries, and tips for avoiding the charge.

Annual Fee
💰 $0-$695/year
Credit Cards

Yearly charge for holding a credit card. Call to request retention offers or downgrade.

Application Fee
💰 $25-$75
Rental Housing, Loans

Non-refundable fee to process applications. Ask about waivers.

Assessment Fee
💰 $50-$500+
HOA, Real Estate

Fee for common-area maintenance. Review HOA documents before purchase.

Brokerage Fee
💰 $0-$30/trade
Investments

Commission for executing trades. Use commission-free brokers.

Closing Cost
💰 2%-5% of price
Real Estate

Fees for real estate closing. Shop lenders and negotiate.

Dispatch Fee
💰 $25-$100
HVAC, Plumbing

Charge for sending a technician. Ask if included in service call.

Delivery Fee
💰 $5-$50
Retail, E-commerce

Charge for transporting goods. Look for free delivery minimums.

Enrollment Fee
💰 $25-$200
Gyms, Memberships

One-time fee to join. Ask for waivers during promotions.

12b-1 Fee
💰 0.25%–1% of assets annually
Investment/Mutual Funds

An annual fee deducted from mutual fund assets for marketing and distribution costs. Choose no-load funds to avoid.

Administrative Fee
💰 $5–$50/month
Banking, Insurance, HOA

Covers general overhead. Request itemization and negotiate caps.

Arbitration Fee
💰 $200–$2,000+
Consumer Contracts

Cost of resolving disputes through arbitration. Review contracts for mandatory clauses.

Balance Transfer Fee
💰 3%–5% of amount
Credit Cards

Fee for moving debt between cards. Use 0% fee promotions.

Booking Fee
💰 $5–$50
Travel, Events

Service charge by third-party booking sites. Book directly to avoid.

Cancellation Fee
💰 $25–$500
Travel, Gyms, Telecom

Penalty for canceling a reservation or contract. Read cancellation policies before booking.

Cash Advance Fee
💰 3%–5% ($5 min)
Credit Cards

Fee for withdrawing cash against credit. Use debit cards instead.

Change Order Fee
💰 $50–$500
Construction, Renovation

Charge for modifying contract scope. Get all changes in writing with pricing.

Convenience Fee
💰 2%–4% of payment
Utilities, Government

Fee for paying by credit card or online. Pay by ACH or check to avoid.

Data Overage Fee
💰 $10–$20/GB
Telecom, Internet

Charge for exceeding data caps. Monitor usage and switch to unlimited plans.

Diagnostic Fee
💰 $50–$200
Auto Repair, HVAC

Fee for evaluating a problem. Ask if waived if you approve the repair.

Documentation Fee
💰 $150–$1,000
Auto Sales, Lending

Fee for processing paperwork. Actual cost under $50. Negotiate removal.

Early Termination Fee
💰 $50–$400+
Telecom, Leases, SaaS

Penalty for ending a contract early. Choose month-to-month plans.

Emergency Service Fee
💰 $100–$500+
HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical

Premium for after-hours service. Plan routine maintenance to avoid emergencies.

Equipment Rental Fee
💰 $5–$20/month
Telecom, Healthcare

Recurring charge for leased equipment. Buy your own to eliminate.

Escalation Clause
💰 5%–25% increase
Construction, Leases

Allows automatic price increases. Request fixed-price contracts.

Facility Fee
💰 $100–$2,000+
Healthcare

Charge for hospital-owned facility use. Ask for outpatient clinic options.

Foreign Transaction Fee
💰 1%–3%
Credit Cards, Banking

Fee for international purchases. Use fee-free travel cards.

GAP Insurance
💰 $300–$900
Auto Sales

Covers loan balance if car is totaled. Buy from insurer ($20-40/yr) not dealer.

Inactivity Fee
💰 $2–$15/month
Banking, Prepaid Cards

Charge for dormant accounts. Make a small transaction every few months.

Junk Fee
💰 $1–$100+
All Industries

Deceptive fee with no legitimate service. Ask bluntly "can you remove this?"

Late Payment Fee
💰 $25–$50
Credit Cards, Loans, Rent

Penalty for missing payment due date. Set up auto-pay to avoid.

Loan Packing
💰 $500–$5,000+
Auto Loans, Personal Loans

Adding unnecessary products to loans without consent. Read every document.

Market Adjustment
💰 $1,000–$20,000
Auto Sales

Dealer markup above MSRP. Refuse to pay; expand search radius.

Monthly Maintenance Fee
💰 $5–$30/month
Banking, Software

Recurring account fee. Choose no-fee accounts.

NSF Fee
💰 $25–$40
Banking

Fee for declined transactions. Link savings account for overdraft protection.

Origination Fee
💰 0.5%–2% of loan
Mortgages, Personal Loans

Upfront loan processing fee. Compare across lenders.

Overdraft Fee
💰 $25–$45
Banking

Fee when bank covers overspend. Opt out of overdraft coverage.

Processing Fee
💰 $2–$25 or 1%–3%
Banking, Insurance, Loans

Catch-all administrative charge. Ask what specific activity it covers.

Regulatory Recovery Fee
💰 $0.50–$5/month
Telecom, Utilities

Company-imposed charge (not a government tax). Compare across providers.

Renewal Premium
💰 20%–100% increase
Insurance, Subscriptions

Higher rate upon renewal. Shop around before renewing.

Resort Fee
💰 $15–$60/night
Hospitality/Travel

Mandatory daily charge not in advertised rate. Call hotel for total price.

Service Charge
💰 3%–20% of total
Hospitality, Events

General service fee. Ask what it covers.

Setup Fee
💰 $25–$200
Telecom, Banking, SaaS

One-time account establishment charge. Ask for waiver during promotion.

Stop Payment Fee
💰 $15–$35
Banking

Fee to stop a check. Use online bill pay to avoid checks.

Termination Fee
💰 $50–$500+
Telecom, Leases, SaaS

Penalty for ending an agreement. Read termination clauses before signing.

Transaction Fee
💰 $0.25–$5 or 1%–3%
Banking, Investments

Per-transaction processing charge. Batch transactions to minimize.

Upcoding
💰 $100–$10,000+
Medical Billing

Billing for expensive procedure than performed. Review Explanation of Benefits carefully.

VIN Etching Fee
💰 $100–$500
Auto Sales

Window etching for theft deterrence. DIY kit costs $20. Decline at dealer.

Wire Transfer Fee
💰 $15–$50
Banking, Real Estate

Fee for electronic funds transfer. Use ACH or Zelle for lower-cost transfers.
