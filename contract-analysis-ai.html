How Contract Analysis AI Works

Contract analysis AI uses natural language processing and machine learning to scan your documents for hidden fees, pricing risks, and unfavorable terms. Unlike simple keyword search tools, HiddenFeeAI reads your contract in context—understanding how clauses work together, where costs are hidden, and what terms need renegotiation before you sign.

The AI analyzes eight dimensions of your contract: pricing structure, hidden fees, clause risk, language balance, scope definitions, payment schedules, regulatory compliance, and negotiation opportunities. Each dimension is evaluated against industry benchmarks and known risk patterns, giving you a complete picture of what you are signing.

What HiddenFeeAI Finds That Other Tools Miss

Most contract analysis tools only find what you tell them to look for. HiddenFeeAI discovers things you didn't know to search for. It catches administrative fees disguised as legitimate charges, change-order clauses written to maximize contractor profit, payment schedules that leave you financially exposed, and warranty limitations buried in dense legal paragraphs.

In one recent analysis, a homeowner uploaded a $65,000 renovation contract. The AI found three hidden issues: a 35% markup on materials (industry standard is 10-15%), an automatic renewal clause that would have locked them into a maintenance contract at $400/month, and a change-order provision with no pricing cap.
