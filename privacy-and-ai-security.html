Privacy and Data Handling

DetectHiddenFees takes your privacy seriously. This page explains how we handle your documents during AI analysis.

Encrypted Transmission

Documents are encrypted using TLS 1.2+ during upload and download — the same standard used by banks and financial institutions.

No AI Training

Your documents are never used to train or improve AI models. Each analysis is a one-time, private processing event. No document content is retained for model improvement.

Automatic Deletion

Uploaded documents are scheduled for deletion from our systems after analysis completes. Analysis results are kept for up to 30 days, then also scheduled for deletion.

No Data Sharing

We never share, sell, or license your documents to third parties. No advertisers, no data brokers, no external services. Your data stays yours.

Access Controls

No employee or contractor can view your uploaded documents. Access is limited to the automated processing pipeline only. All access is logged and audited.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your analysis results before the 30-day retention period ends by contacting us.

For complete details, read our Data Handling Policy, Security Overview, or Terms of Service.
