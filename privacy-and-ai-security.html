Privacy and AI Security: How Your Documents Are Protected

Your privacy matters. This page explains how we handle your documents during AI analysis, what data we collect, how long we keep it, and who has access to it.

🔒

Encrypted Transmission

Documents are encrypted with TLS 1.2+ during upload and download

🗑️

Automatic Deletion

Documents are scheduled for removal after analysis completes

🚫

No AI Training

Your documents are never used to train or improve AI models

🔐

Limited Access

No employee or third party can view your uploaded documents

Privacy-Focused Design

DetectHiddenFees was built with a privacy-first approach. Our platform is designed to process your documents without retaining them longer than necessary and without exposing them to unauthorized parties.

Because we process financial documents that often contain sensitive personal information, we have implemented industry-standard security practices to protect your data throughout the analysis process.

Secure Transmission

When you upload a document to HiddenFeeAI, the data is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher during transmission. This is the same encryption protocol used by commercial websites and online services to protect data in transit. The document is encrypted from the moment it leaves your device until it reaches our processing systems.

Document Usage

Your documents are used for a single purpose: to perform the AI analysis you requested. Specifically:

  • Documents are processed by our AI analysis engine to identify hidden fees, pricing risks, and unfavorable terms
  • Documents are never used for AI model training or system improvement
  • Document contents are never sold, licensed, or shared with third parties
  • Analysis results are provided to you and are not accessible to any other user

Retention and Deletion

We follow a transparent retention policy:

  • During processing: Documents are held temporarily in a controlled environment
  • After analysis: Documents are scheduled for deletion from active systems
  • Analysis results: Retained for up to 30 days, then scheduled for deletion
  • Backups: No document data is included in system backups

Data Sharing

DetectHiddenFees does not sell your personal information or document data. We do not share uploaded documents with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties. The AI analysis is performed using our own infrastructure. For complete details, see our Data Handling Policy and Terms of Service.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Because documents are automatically deleted after analysis, the information available is limited to your analysis results. To request deletion of your analysis results before the standard 30-day retention period, or for any other privacy-related requests, please contact us.

Questions About Privacy

If you have questions about our privacy practices that are not addressed here, please review our Data Handling Policy or contact us directly. We are committed to transparency about how we handle your data.
