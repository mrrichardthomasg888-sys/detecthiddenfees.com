How DetectHiddenFees Analyzes Documents

Our AI analysis methodology is built on five stages of forensic document processing. Each stage adds a layer of scrutiny that transforms raw document text into actionable intelligence about hidden fees, pricing risks, and negotiation opportunities. View our complete methodology →

1

Document Extraction

The AI receives and digitizes your financial document using OCR technology, preserving all line items, pricing, fees, and contract language exactly as written. The system verifies completeness before proceeding to analysis.

2

Fee Pattern Recognition

The system scans for known hidden fee patterns including vague fee descriptions, above-market markups, duplicate charges, and deceptive pricing language commonly used across construction, healthcare, automotive, and banking industries.

3

Pricing Anomaly Detection

Each charge is compared against market benchmarks to identify pricing that exceeds typical ranges for the specific industry and geographic area. The AI flags charges that deviate from expected norms with specific variance data.

4

Contract Language Analysis

The AI evaluates contract terms for risky clauses including automatic renewal provisions, price escalation terms, broad discretionary pricing authority, and language that shifts cost risk to consumers without clear disclosure.

5

Risk Explanation Generation

Each finding is converted into a plain-language explanation with specific questions to ask, market context for comparison, and actionable negotiation leverage points that consumers can use directly with providers.
